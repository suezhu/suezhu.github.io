Learning from previous two failures, we decided to drive to Iron Horses Monument in east Washington. It should be the perfect spot to watch Neowise which would be in northern west sky early evening after sunset.

This time I was getting nervous about the trip. I downloaded 6 applications to help identify the location of Neowise. We started driving there around 2:00PM. On the way there, I had been playing with my 6 applications to make sure.

The sky was so pretty and it was a splendid show of sun rise. We did enjoy a rare sun rise! The “rare” is for us since we are always sleep late.

My first attempt to see Neowise ended up with a sun rise show!
