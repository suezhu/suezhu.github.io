This Memorial Weekend we went to camping at Antelope Island. It was a beautiful campground. We had a great time there.

There were so much salt everywhere. People here put the water into fields and let water dissolve the salt from soil. Then when the water evaporated, white salt will appear!



This was the place I dreamed about a lot. I planned to get into the water. But it was forbidden now! 😳
