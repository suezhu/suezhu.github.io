Deception Pass, WA


Just completed our camping trip in Deception Pass.

We went there with our new toy, SylvanSport GO. It was very pleasant. I found that I did sleep a lot, average 10 hours a night. One night, I slept 12 hours!!! The evenings were cold, rained a little one evening. However with the new toy, we slept comfortably and dry.

The weather was nice and the park is great. There’s lake and ocean. We met many little birds, did fishing (no catching) and enjoyed splendid sunset!!!!

I tried to roast egg on the campfire for the three nights. The eggs were all burst/broken. They did taste good although they didn’t look good.
