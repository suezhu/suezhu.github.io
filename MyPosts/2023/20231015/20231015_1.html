Annular Solar Eclipse

The sky was full of clouds! We all hoped that the wind would blow away the clouds. When it was the time, there was a tiny little hole in the clouds. We saw the 100% annular solar eclipse! It was very short, 2 seconds!! Everyone was very excited and we were all cheering. Nobody remembered to take photos!!!

Then we all paid very close attention to clouds. We would cheer if we could take a glimpse! The clouds moved from right to left. Hence the crowd at our right would see the hole before we did and they would cheer. We noticed their cheering then we saw the little hole and sun. We cheered to notify the crowd at our left. The crowd at our left would cheer to notify the crowd at their left! This reminded me about the cheering at a game!! It was fun!!!

We stayed there more than 2 hours and we only saw sun 5 times with 2-5 seconds each time. In total, we only saw sun for last than 1 minutes!!!



This was the second time of the little hole in the clouds to provide a quick glimpse of the eclipse! I was not the loudest in the video. I was the second loudest!😜
