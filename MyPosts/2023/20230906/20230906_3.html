Mono Lake, CA
1 2 3 4 5 6


Mono Lake is a salt lake in the mountains. The water here is 3 times saltier than sea water! I did put a water bottle and it floated! I also tasted the water - horribly salty and bitter!!! 😏

All the formations we saw now used to be under salty water. It was decided to draw the water here to supply Los Angeles. The water level is 25 feet below it was.
