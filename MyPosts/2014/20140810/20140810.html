Valley Camp, WA


This weekend we went to camp. We arrived at Friday evening and we setup tent when it was dark already. And someone was even later than ours. We finished camping on Sunday afternoon. We had a good time. Little deer also dropped by to peek at what we were doing. We roasted marshmallows at campfire, played water gun fight. We enjoyed the supermoon at evening. I felt that it was too short. I needed more time to relax.
